A Word Of Advice

Transitioning from high school to university requires a significant adjustment in learning strategies. Unlike high school, where teachers provide comprehensive instruction and guidance, university lecturers primarily focus on delivering lectures that outline the course structure and key concepts. Students are expected to take ownership of their learning by actively engaging with the material, conducting thorough research, and utilizing the vast resources available online. While academic achievement is important, it's crucial to expand your horizons beyond merely passing exams.
